Academic Mentality

I believe fostering a profound sense of ambition, in conjunction with a genuine care and passion for learning and for the development of the student as a human being. Study should not be about mindless pushing and a gamble on results. I offer insight into how to turn study into a wholehearted activity that is engaging, fulfilling, and rewarding on a personal level.

Many tutoring companies, I have found, do not actually offer individual student support, and simply supply extra resources that can only push the student so far. I do not plan to place extra, unnecessary burdens on students’ shoulders. Rather, I aim to offer strategy and extension that empowers the student to confidently and proudly execute to the best of their true ability.
